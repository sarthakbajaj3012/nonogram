ABOUT THE GAME!

  • A dark gray block is an unmarked block
  • A green block depicts an empty block
  • A red block depicts a filled block
  • Left click on the block to mark it as filled. Note : It cannot be reversed!
  • Right click on the block to toggle between empty and unmarked.
  • You will have four lives in each level
  • Beside each row of the grid are listed the lengths of the runs of black squares on that row.
  • Above each column are listed the lengths of the runs of black squares in that column.
  • There are three levels in the game
  • You can only access the next level after completing the previous levels
  • A red cross indicates that you have filled a wrong block and hence you will loose one life
